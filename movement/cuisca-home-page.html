Two Paths, One Philosophy

CuISCA represents a unique dual mission, applying the same core principles to both community advocacy and sacred ceremony work:

The Movement

As a pacifist collective, we're dedicated to demonstrating that security without violence is possible. We provide training in mediation, de-escalation, and community-building, fostering resilience through cooperation based on shared resources and mutual support.

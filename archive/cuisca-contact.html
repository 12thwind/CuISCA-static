Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get involved with CuISCA if I'm new to the area?

The best way to get started is to attend one of our regular community gatherings such as the weekly meditation circle or monthly potluck. These events are designed to be welcoming to newcomers and provide natural opportunities to meet community members. Check our Community Practices page for upcoming events.

Do I need to have previous spiritual practice experience to participate?

Not at all! Our community welcomes people at all stages of their spiritual journey, including those who are just beginning to explore. All practices include guidance for beginners, and we cultivate a culture where questions and learning are valued.

How far in advance should I book a ceremony?

For weddings during peak season (June-September), we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For other ceremonies or off-season dates, 3-6 months is usually sufficient. However, we occasionally have availability for shorter timeframes, so don't hesitate to inquire even if your timeline is compressed.

Is CuISCA affiliated with a particular religious tradition?

No, we are not affiliated with any specific religious tradition. Our approach draws wisdom from various spiritual paths while honoring each person's unique relationship with the sacred. Our community includes people from diverse spiritual backgrounds as well as those who don't identify with any particular tradition.

Are your events family-friendly?

Many of our gatherings are appropriate for families, particularly our seasonal celebrations and community potlucks. Some meditation practices and ceremonies are designed primarily for adults, though we occasionally offer specific children's programming. Events that are especially suitable for families are noted in our calendar listings.

Have a question not answered here? Feel free to reach out using the contact form below.

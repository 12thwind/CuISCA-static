Ways to Engage

Wedding Ceremonies

Our primary service is creating and officiating meaningful wedding ceremonies that honor your relationship and connection to the Keweenaw landscape. Each ceremony is thoughtfully crafted to reflect your unique journey and values.

Initial consultations are offered at no charge to explore possibilities and determine if our approach aligns with your vision. While we don't provide event planning services, we can refer you to trusted local planners who know the area well.

Personal Guidance

For those seeking to deepen their connection with the Keweenaw and incorporate mindful practices into daily life, we offer individual consultations and resources tailored to your specific interests and needs.

Whether you're a long-time resident or new to the area, these conversations can help you develop a more meaningful relationship with this remarkable landscape and integrate our philosophical principles into your everyday experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I engage with CuISCA if I'm new to the area?

The best way to connect is through a personal consultation to discuss your interest in our wedding officiant services or guidance around living mindfully in the Keweenaw. You can reach out using our contact form below to schedule a conversation.

Do I need to have previous spiritual practice experience to work with you?

Not at all! Our approach welcomes people at all stages of their spiritual journey, including those who are just beginning to explore. Our guidance is accessible for beginners, and we cultivate an approach where questions and personal exploration are valued.

How far in advance should I book a wedding ceremony?

For weddings during peak season (June-September), we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For off-season dates, 3-6 months is usually sufficient. However, we occasionally have availability for shorter timeframes, so don't hesitate to inquire even if your timeline is compressed.

Is CuISCA affiliated with a particular religious tradition?

No, we are not affiliated with any specific religious tradition. Our approach draws wisdom from various spiritual paths while honoring each person's unique relationship with the sacred. We work with couples and individuals from diverse spiritual backgrounds as well as those who don't identify with any particular tradition.

Can you recommend event planners for our wedding in the Keweenaw?

Yes! While we focus on creating and officiating meaningful ceremonies rather than event planning, we maintain relationships with trusted local planners who understand the unique considerations of the Keweenaw region. We're happy to provide referrals to planners who align with your specific needs and vision.
